Here is a comprehensive list of the best websites, fan sites, articles, and resources dedicated to the FASA Star Trek RPG (from the 1980s era). I've focused on unique domains, prioritizing those with substantial content like rules, homebrews, reviews, modules, starship designs, and community discussions. Where a site has multiple relevant pages, I've selected a representative URL. These are drawn from extensive web searches and site explorations for quality and relevance.

### Dedicated Fan Sites and Resource Hubs
- **https://fasaststcs.com/**: A major fan site with starship stats, scenarios, counters, house rules, and resources for the Starship Tactical Combat Simulator (STCS) aspect of the game.
- **http://jt-sw.com/ststcs/**: Fan projects including character creation tools, starship combat aids, and custom content for FASA gameplay.
- **https://gsllcblog.com/fasa-star-trek-rpg-resources**: Collection of fan-made tools like character builders, databases, rules summaries, and prep aids for GMs and players.
- **https://www.jackphoton.space/**: Fan project developing a "3rd edition" of the FASA rules, with updates and expansions.
- **https://www.morenashipyards.com/**: Archive for the Morena Shipyards email list, featuring fan starship designs and collaborative projects.
- **https://www.ststcsolda.space/**: Focused on the STCS game, with databases, archives, and fan expansions for starship combat.
- **https://ufc465537.neocities.org/**: Detailed fan site with regular updates, including resources, indexes, and custom content for the RPG.
- **https://vintagestarships.wordpress.com/**: Fan site with starship designs, house rules, construction tables, and FASA-specific content.

### Community Forums and Discussion Boards
- **https://oldschooltrek.proboards.com/**: Forum dedicated to TOS-era gaming, with a active section for FASA discussions, homebrews, and sharing.
- **https://ststcsolda.proboards.com/**: Message board tied to the STCS online database, for fan archives, tactics, and community support.
- **http://www.trek-rpg.net/oldsite/**: Older message board covering multiple Trek RPGs, with a dedicated FASA section for threads and files.
- **https://forum.rpg.net/index.php?threads/isttroko-fasa-star-trek-adventure-modules-whats-good-what-to-avoid.910432/**: RPG.net forum thread reviewing and recommending FASA adventure modules.
- **https://www.reddit.com/r/rpg/comments/1ap1m98/for_those_that_played_it_thoughts_on_fasas_old**: Reddit discussion on player experiences and thoughts about the game.
- **https://www.fantasygrounds.com/forums/showthread.php?14284-Thinking-about-FASA-Star-Trek-to-BRP**: Fantasy Grounds forum thread on converting FASA rules to other systems.
- **https://www.facebook.com/groups/ststcsolda/posts/3825350597505718**: Facebook group post offering PDF copies and discussions for FASA materials.
