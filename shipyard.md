A growing collection of 3d printable models of FASA and related ships, models can be viewed in 3d and printed on a suitable 3d printer. [Free printing guide](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1c8KXfu4-6gyc6lvXLAPbvnhgGBXnLfBW/view?usp=drive_link) for SLA printing.

### Captain Mojo 
- [Captain Mojo on Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/CaptainMojoShipyards) please support this amazing designer.
- [Captain Mojo on Cults 3d](https://cults3d.com/en/users/Captain_Mojo/3d-models) free FASA models to print.
- [Captain Mojo on Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1W5B4TEpE54ax9C5oGcD6SvcOxbK5TIcj) free FASA models to print and [printing guide](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1c8KXfu4-6gyc6lvXLAPbvnhgGBXnLfBW/view?usp=drive_link).

### FASA Fan
- [FASA Fan on Cults 3d](https://cults3d.com/en/users/FASAfan/3d-models) contains models of outposts and stations for modest fee.
